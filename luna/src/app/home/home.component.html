About Us:

As both a desktop and mobile web application, Luna is helping users stay organized and educated, and improve awareness about menstruation.

Luna's purpose is to increase access to helpful advice about menstruation, provide a means of tracking period-related events, and destigmatize menstrual care.

Within the application, you can find information about experiencing a first period, menstruation cycles, puberty, birth control options, and more.

Luna's Importance:

Luna is an application developed to support individuals who experience menstruation with factual information and valuable tools. Luna's potential to beneficially impact its users is explained through data visualizations shown below. These diagrams are based upon a recent study on the prevalence of menstrual pain. The conclusions drawn from the research experiment highlight the importance of being educated about menstruation and its related symptoms.

Figure 1: Pain Onset vs. Menarche

Menarche is the first menstrual period that an individual may experience. The data visualization illustrated in Figure 1 shows that individuals who are on their first period typically experience menstrual pain during this time, while others will experience this pain within the coming year. Therefore, it's important for young individuals to be educated on menstruation, so they can better handle any negative symptoms that may be encountered during their menarche and the immediate time after.

Figure 2: Effects of Periods on Performance/Absences of Individuals

The data visualization illustrated in Figure 2 shows that approximately half of the individuals who were in the case study experienced an increase in absenteeism and a decrease in performance both socially and academically. Knowing more about menstrual flow and the symptoms associated with it can help individuals avoid having to miss important events. It's clear that the ability to manage the negative side effects that are correlated with periods can benefit individuals on a monthly basis.
