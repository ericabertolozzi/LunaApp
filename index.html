About Us:

At its core, Luna is a one-stop location to improve awareness and knowledge of menstruation for both the young and old. Luna's purpose is to increase access to helpful information about menstruation, provide young people with a means of tracking period-related events and destigmatize menstrual care by providing factual information. Within the application, you can find helpful information about experiencing a first period, the changes that come along with puberty, birth control options, and more.
